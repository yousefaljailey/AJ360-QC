Complete QC Suite

A Complete Suite of QC to Start Your Workflow

Every check your broadcast team needs — video, audio, captions, metadata, and container — in a single unified platform built for newsroom speed.

  • Video & Audio Analysis

    Deep-frame analysis covering codec, bitrate, loudness, levels, silence, clipping, and phase issues.

  • Caption & Subtitle Validation

    Verify CEA-608, CEA-708, TTML, and SRT tracks meet broadcast requirements.

  • Profile-Based Checking

    Apply custom QC profiles per broadcaster, platform, or delivery standard — EBU, ATSC, DVB, and more.

  • Instant PDF Reports

    Export detailed QC reports with timecodes and thumbnails for every issue found.
